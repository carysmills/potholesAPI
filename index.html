Look out for potholes,

See potholes recently

This site uses the City of Toronto’s Open311 API, which retrieves service requests for potholes and graffiti calls. It pulls 1,000 calls or all records from the past 90 days, whichever is smaller. The results have been filtered here to only show pothole files that have a status other than closed, meaning pothole files with an open or null status are displayed.
